Loop ████ - Day 0

You are ██████; you’ve been stuck in a loop for a while now.

Your only way out is to fill the silver key with knowledge. In order to do that, you must view logs of the Sekai Spaceship in every loop and figure out its fate.

Amongst the Sekai Spaceship’s crew of ████, ████ are infected by the GNOS.

The GNOSIA’s goal is to eliminate humanity; it will target a human once per day.

Every day, the crew will gather and decide which passengers to put into Cold Sleep; the loop ends when no Gnosia presence is detected, or when the GNOSIA takes over the ship.

If you wish to escape this loop, you must find out who the GNOSIA were.

Good luck.
