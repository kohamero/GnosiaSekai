Loop ████ - Day 0

You are Ichika, the navigator of the sekai spaceship.

Amongst your crew of nine, two are infected by the GNOSIA.

The GNOSIA's goal is to eliminate humanity; it will target a HUMAN once per day.

Every day, the crew will gather and decide two suspicious passengers to put into Cold Sleep.

It is your duty, as the navigator, to be the deciding factor and eliminate the threat. If you fail to do so, your ship will be taken over by the GNOSIA and you will be eliminated.


A human will never lie; some have a special role that can help you survive.


This time, your spaceship has detected three unique roles :

Engineer - Human role, can choose one person per day to confirm and report human/GNOSIA Status.

Doctor - Human role, confirms and reports human/GNOSIA Status of those put into cold sleep.

Guardian angel - Human role, can protect one selected person from GNOSIA attacks.


Your role: Guardian Angel
